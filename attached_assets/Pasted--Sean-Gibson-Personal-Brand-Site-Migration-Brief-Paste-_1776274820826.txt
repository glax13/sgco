## Brand Reference

**Visual identity**
- Background: `#07111a`
- Surface: `#0a1520`
- Card: `#0c1927`
- Cream: `#EAE4D6`
- Green: `#2EB872`
- Fonts: Inter (body/headings) + DM Serif Display (italic serif accents only)
- Grain texture overlay via SVG fractalNoise at 3% opacity
- No em dashes anywhere in copy

**Palette logic**
- Cream used at 4 opacity levels: solid, 60%, 25%, 7%
- Green used at 4 levels: solid, 40%, 10%, stroke-only
- Green is accent only — never used for large backgrounds

**Motion principles**
- Page transitions: `cubic-bezier(.16,1,.3,1)` rise animation
- Hover: `translateY(-1px)` on buttons, directional line reveals on cards
- Ticker strip auto-scrolls, pauses on hover
- Each interactive element has a distinct animation axis
